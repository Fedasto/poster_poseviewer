# üì± poster_poseviewer

**Real-time photogrammetry streamed directly from your smartphone**

poster_poseviewer enables live capture of images or video frames from a
mobile device and streams them to a photogrammetry backend for
**real-time 3D reconstruction**.\
The tool is designed for **experimentation, research, academic demos,
and rapid testing**, with a special focus on **scientific poster
sessions and conference presentations**.

**Version:** 1.0\
**Developed for:** BicoQ Poster Competition
